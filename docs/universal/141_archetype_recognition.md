## Key Insight

"Is this a lifecycle phase pattern?" is analytically interesting but has LOW VOI - you adapt similarly either way.

## Common Archetypes

Recognizing these patterns unlocks their known solutions:

**Coordination Problems:**
- Tragedy of the Commons → regulation, privatization, or community norms
- Chicken-and-egg → bootstrapping, subsidizing one side
- Prisoner's Dilemma → repeated games, reputation, punishment
- Coordination Game → focal points, communication, standards

**Structural Problems:**
- Hub dependency → redundancy or hub strengthening
- Single point of failure → redundancy
- Rate-limiting step → bottleneck focus
- Circular dependency → bootstrapping or decoupling

**Incentive Problems:**
- Principal-agent → monitoring, alignment, skin in game
- Moral hazard → risk sharing, incentive design
- Adverse selection → signaling, screening

**The move:** Before analyzing from first principles, check if the situation matches a known archetype. Patterns have proven solutions.
