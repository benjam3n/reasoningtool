**Source**: Derived from space discovery Step 2 - stakeholder perspectives
**Structure**: One question per entry, VOI-marked with realistic distribution

---

## VOI Rating = Action Divergence

- **HIGH**: Different answers → completely different action paths
- **MED**: Different answers → same general direction, different approach
- **LOW**: Different answers → same actions, different framing/flavor

## Key Insight

**VOI ≠ Stakeholder Importance**. A stakeholder can be important but LOW VOI if their existence doesn't change your actions.

**VOI = Action Divergence**. HIGH VOI means: if you're wrong about this stakeholder, you'll take completely different actions.
