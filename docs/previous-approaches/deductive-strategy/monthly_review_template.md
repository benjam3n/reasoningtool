## Appendix: Question Bank for Monthly Deep Dive

Select one or let it emerge from the month's themes:

**Foundational:**
- What am I assuming that I haven't questioned?
- What would I do differently if I had 10x resources? 1/10th?
- What am I avoiding thinking about?
- What would someone I respect do in my situation?

**Goal-related:**
- Why do I want what I want?
- What would actually satisfy me?
- Am I pursuing the goal or avoiding something?
- What's the cost of achieving this goal?

**Strategy-related:**
- What's the weakest link in my current approach?
- What would make this obviously fail?
- What's a completely different approach?
- Who has solved a similar problem?

**Value-related:**
- What would I regret not doing?
- What matters that I'm neglecting?
- What am I sacrificing and is it worth it?
- How would I advise a friend in my situation?

**Meta:**
- Is my thinking clear or confused?
- Am I being honest with myself?
- What am I in denial about?
- What would change my mind?
